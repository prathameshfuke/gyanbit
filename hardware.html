THE PCB

Inside the GyanBit

A carefully engineered, 4-layer FR4 board housing standard protocols to teach hardware fundamentals without overwhelming complexity.

MICROCONTROLLER
Raspberry Pi RP2040

Dual-core ARM Cortex-M0+ running MicroPython at 133MHz.

DISPLAY (I2C)
1.3" OLED (SH1106)

Monochrome 128x64 display driven via hardware I2C at 400kHz.

AUDIO (PWM)
PAM8403 Amplifier

3W Class-D amp driving an 8-ohm mini speaker directly via PWM.

STORAGE
2MB QSPI Flash

Holds the MicroPython firmware and generated `main.py` files.

POWER
3.7V / USB-C

Runs off USB-C or an internal single-cell Li-Po battery.

INPUT
6× Tactile Buttons

D-Pad (Up, Down, Left, Right), A, and B hooked to digital inputs.
